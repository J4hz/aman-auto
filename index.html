Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Passion

Founded in Nairobi in 2012, Aman Auto has grown from a small yard in Industrial Area to one of Kenya's most respected automotive dealerships. We specialize in quality pre-owned and brand-new vehicles, catering to both individual buyers and corporate fleets.

"Our mission is simple — to make premium car ownership accessible to every Kenyan, with honesty and dignity at the core of every transaction."

We believe every Kenyan deserves a reliable, safe vehicle — whether you're navigating Nairobi's CBD, conquering the Great Rift Valley, or cruising the coastal highway.
